About Us

Who are we

The Scrumptious Slice is more than just a home bakery — it’s a celebration of homemade goodness, heartfelt flavors, and joyful moments shared over dessert. Born in a cozy kitchen filled with the smell of freshly baked cakes and warm cookies, this little venture began with a simple idea: to make people smile, one bite at a time.

What started with a few cake orders for close friends and family quickly turned into a full-blown passion project. From birthday cakes to festive hampers, every item is made with love, care, and an obsession for quality. We don’t believe in shortcuts — just real ingredients, small batches, and big flavor.

Today, The Scrumptious Slice is proud to be a part of your birthdays, celebrations, and everyday sweet cravings. We’re not a factory, and we never want to be. We’re a heart-led home bakery, and that’s what makes every slice special.

What We Believe

At The Scrumptious Slice, we believe that baking is more than just mixing ingredients — it’s a way of creating joy, comfort, and connection. Each dessert we make carries a little piece of our heart, our time, and our dedication. That’s why we bake everything in small batches, using only high-quality ingredients, and always with a personal touch.

We believe that every order is special. Whether you’re celebrating a birthday, surprising a friend, or simply treating yourself after a long day, your dessert should feel like it was made just for you — because it was. We put time into crafting not just the taste, but also the look, the packaging, and the entire experience.

At our core, we believe in spreading happiness through simple, thoughtful gestures — and there’s no gesture sweeter than dessert. Every cake, cookie, and box that leaves our kitchen is made to bring smiles, start conversations, and create memories.

Meet the baker

Hi there! I’m Nishtha Shethwala, the founder, baker, designer, and daydreamer behind The Scrumptious Slice. Baking has always been more than a hobby for me — it’s a way to express love, creativity, and joy. I still remember the excitement of baking my first cake, experimenting with frosting techniques, and watching someone’s face light up with the first bite.

I started this journey with no big business plan — just a passion for desserts and a desire to share something meaningful with others. Slowly, through word of mouth and loyal support, what was once a side project became something much bigger than I imagined.

I bake every order myself, in my little kitchen, and I pour my heart into every batch. From designing custom cakes to packing gift boxes, I try to make each experience feel personal and memorable. For me, it’s not just about desserts — it’s about being part of your happiest moments, your comfort days, and your celebrations.
